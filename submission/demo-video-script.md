# Demo video script — 3:00, screen recording + phone camera

**Setup before recording:** dashboard open on South Delhi with seeded data;
phone with WhatsApp sandbox joined; screen recorder capturing both (phone
via scrcpy/QuickTime, or film the phone over-the-shoulder). Quiet room.

---

**0:00–0:20 — Hook (voiceover over a photo of letters/queue at an office)**
"An MP represents twenty-five lakh people. Their needs arrive as letters,
phone calls, and pressure — and the loudest voice wins. sunwai puts every
voice on the record instead."

**0:20–0:55 — The citizen (phone screen)**
Record a real WhatsApp voice note in Hindi: broken streetlight, DON'T name
the area. Show the formatted reply landing: recorded ✅, ref number, and the
bot asking "which area/colony?" Reply with a VOICE note: "Govindpuri gali 4".
Show "स्थान दर्ज / Location noted: Govind Puri."
Voiceover: "No app. No form. Any language. Voice, photo, or text — and the
bot asks for what's missing."

**0:55–1:30 — The engine (dashboard, Hotspot Map)**
Show the submission appearing on the map (circle grows). Click the drainage
hotspot: 47 submissions, quotes in three languages, one demand.
Voiceover: "Gemini transcribes, categorizes, and geolocates every message.
The same complaint in Hindi, English, or Bangla becomes ONE demand with real
count — that's demand, deduplicated."

**1:30–2:05 — Evidence & ranking (Ranked Works)**
Expand №1. Read the formula aloud: submissions × trend × evidence gap.
Point at the Census facts and the sourced ward number, then the AI-written
justification. Voiceover: "Every rank is defensible in public — the formula
and the government data are on the screen."

**2:05–2:30 — Silent Needs + multi-constituency**
Show Madangir flagged ("44% SC, resettlement colony, zero submissions —
suggest field visit"). Then switch the FILE selector to Mumbai North East:
Marathi demands, new map. Voiceover: "It also notices who ISN'T speaking.
And a new constituency is one config file — here's Mumbai, in Marathi."

**2:30–3:00 — Transparency + close (public board, MP brief)**
Open /board: being heard vs resolved. Click Export MP Brief, show the
one-pager. Voiceover: "The record the MP sees is the record the public
sees. sunwai — हर आवाज़ दर्ज. Every voice, on the record."

---

**Recording tips:** 1080p minimum; hide bookmarks bar; record the WhatsApp
segment twice (once as backup); keep raw clips — hack2skill sometimes asks
for a longer cut later.
